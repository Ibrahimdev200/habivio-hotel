Accommodation Guidelines

Guests must return apartments in the same condition as received, including furniture, bedding, cutlery, and crockery.

Furniture must not be moved. Fees will apply for missing or damaged items.

Check-in: From 2 PM.
Check-out: By 10 AM, unless otherwise arranged in writing with management.

Bedding, towels, pillows, and blankets are provided unless otherwise requested by the guest.

In compliance with Product Safety Australia guidelines, children under nine years old should not sleep near the edge of a bed.

Occupancy must not exceed:
One-bedroom units: 3 guests.
Two-bedroom units: 6 guests.
Overcrowding may result in additional fees or termination of the stay.

Pets are allowed in designated pet-friendly properties. Drones are permitted provided they do not cause disturbances to others.

Compliance

Guests are required to conduct themselves in an orderly manner, adhere to Habivio policies, and comply with all applicable laws. Management reserves the right to remove any disruptive or non-compliant guests without liability.

Booking Confirmation

A signed booking confirmation must be submitted within 24 hours of making a reservation.
